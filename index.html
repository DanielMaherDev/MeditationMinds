Who We Are

Meditation Minds is a Galway based group, initally setup for communal meditation in the Galway area. We continue to get together frequently for guided medtiation, and also provide resources here to assist with self guided meditation.

Self-Guided meditation

Breath

The breath is the engine of meditation. We can use our breathing to become present, to calm our nervous system, and as a focus point when looking to get into a meditative state

Breathing techniques

Body Scan

Body scans allow you to listen to your body and understand where you may be holding stress. It is a great way to tune in, and become present with yourself.

Release & Reset

Our goal is to make mindfulness and meditation available to everyone, whether you have an hour to spare, or just 5 minutes. We like to belueve that mindfulness and meditation can be used as a tool to reset, and rejuvinate yourself, by giving your body and mind some peace, to replenish however little that is.
