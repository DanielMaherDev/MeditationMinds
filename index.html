GALWAY BASED MEDITATION COMMUNITY

Who We Are

Meditation Minds is a Galway based meditation resources group. Founded, in 2022, Meditation Minds was setup to assist people in beginning their journey with meditation. Using our self guided meditation resources, you should have no issues introducing yourself to the basics.
We also group together for a communal meditation twice a month. If interested in partaking, you can just come on the day mentioned below!
If you have any queries or are interested in learning more, feel free to use out contact us page to reach out.

Self-Guided meditation

Breath

The breath is the engine of meditation. We can use our breathing to become present, to calm our nervous system, and as a focus point when looking to get into a meditative state

Breathing techniques

Body Scan

Body scans allow you to listen to your body and understand where you may be holding stress. It is a great way to tune in, and become present with yourself.

Body Scan techniques

Reset

Our goal is to make mindfulness and meditation available to everyone, whether you have an hour to spare, or just 5 minutes. We like to believe that mindfulness and meditation can be used as a tool to reset, and rejuvinate yourself, by giving your body and mind some peace, to replenish however little that is.
