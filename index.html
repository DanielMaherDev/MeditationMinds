GALWAY BASED MEDITATION COMMUNITY

Breath

Breathe in deeply through your nose for at least three seconds and hold it in for a further two seconds. Next, exhale for at least four seconds through the mouth. You can repeat this exercise a couple of times if you'd like. Next, gradually transition into natural breathing.
